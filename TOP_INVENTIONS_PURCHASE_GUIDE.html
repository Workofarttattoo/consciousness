🔧 Build Schematic (14 Days)
DAY 1-2: GELATION Safety First: Work in ventilated area. Wear gloves and goggles.

1. Mix 500ml sodium silicate + 500ml water in plastic container
2. Dissolve 30g citric acid in 100ml water separately
3. SLOWLY add citric solution to sodium silicate while stirring
4. Monitor pH with test strips (target: 5-6)
5. Stop adding acid when pH hits 5-6
6. Gel forms in 10-60 minutes
7. Cover container (not airtight), let sit 24 hours
DAY 3-5: SOLVENT EXCHANGE Goal: Replace water with ethanol to prevent collapse

Day 3: Pour out expelled water, add ethanol to cover gel, wait 24h
Day 4: Replace with fresh ethanol, wait 24h
Day 5: Final ethanol exchange (now 99%+ pure), wait 24h
DAY 6-9: AMBIENT PRESSURE DRYING The Magic Step: Turn gel into aerogel

1. Setup drying station: heating pad on table, box fan pointing at it
2. Set heating pad to LOW (60°C / 140°F)
3. Remove gel from ethanol, place on heating pad
4. Turn on fan (slow airflow)
5. Surround with silica gel desiccant
6. WAIT 72 hours (ethanol slowly evaporates)
7. Result: Translucent white/blue aerogel (80% transparent)
DAY 10-14: BUILD DISPLAY FRAME 1. Cut acrylic sheets: 2x 24"x24" (front/back), 4x 24"x2" (sides)
2. Drill holes for screws
3. Sandwich aerogel between acrylic sheets (handle gently!)
4. Screw sides together
5. Seal edges with clear tape
6. Mount projector 6 feet behind aerogel
7. Test with checkerboard pattern, adjust focus
8. Project 3D content (YouTube: "hologram content")

• Gel collapsed? Heat too high - reduce to 50°C
