Mission

The 3D3N category focuses on brain-computer interfaces (BCI) that enable Real Virtual Reality. Unlike traditional VR that tricks your eyes with screens, Real VR uses direct brain stimulation to create experiences that are indistinguishable from physical reality.

By stimulating the visual, auditory, and somatosensory cortices while simultaneously decoding motor intent, we can create arbitrary sensory experiences that feel 100% real because your brain cannot distinguish between stimulated and natural neural activity.

How ECH0 Uses 3D3N

1. Continuous Research Scraping
ECH0 queries arXiv across 30+ topics every synthesis session (brain-computer interface, neural prosthetics, virtual reality neuroscience, closed-loop brain stimulation, etc.)

2. Relevance Scoring
Each paper is scored 0.0-1.0 based on keywords. Papers scoring >0.8 are flagged as breakthroughs. High-value keywords include "brain computer interface", "neural implant", "closed-loop", "bidirectional".

3. Sub-Category Classification
Papers are organized into 10 sub-categories (Visual Prosthesis, Neural Decoding, etc.) for targeted synthesis.

4. Cross-Category Synthesis
ECH0 combines papers from different categories to generate novel inventions. Example: Visual Prosthesis + Neural Encoding = Multi-Modal Immersive VR System.

5. Invention Generation
For each synthesis, ECH0 generates a full invention with confidence score, novelty score, Real VR potential assessment, implementation complexity, and technical description.
